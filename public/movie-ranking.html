The Best Movies According to Anthony

My top five movies of all time

The Hobbit

A very fine mediaval movie about courage, brotherhood, and faith

The Lord of the Rings

A sequel to 'The Hobbit'. Really depicted courage and bravery

Pirate of the Carribean

An adventurous and comedy movie

John Wick

An action packed movie. Main theme was vengeance

Jujutsu Kaisen

An action anime with resilient characters
